Compassion for all beings

We are a vegan non-profit animal rescue organization and sanctuary in Ontario, Canada. We rescue and rehabilitate farm animals from slaughterhouses, laboratories, fur farms, and the entertainment industry. We believe that every sentient being has a right to live a life free of suffering and human exploitation.
